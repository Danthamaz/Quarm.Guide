# Hollowshade Moor Zone Event

Hollowshade Moor is home to a three-faction war event involving **Owlbears** (north), **Sonic Wolves** (east), and **Grimlings** (south). Players can manipulate which faction controls which part of the zone by starting and influencing the event.

## Default State

When no event is running, each faction occupies its home territory:

| Location | Default Faction |
|----------|----------------|
| North    | Owlbears       |
| East     | Sonic Wolves   |
| South    | Grimlings      |

## Starting the Event

The event begins when a player kills one of the **faction leaders** at any of the three camps. Killing a leader causes **that leader's faction to be attacked** by a neighboring faction:

| Kill This Leader | Faction Attacked | Attacked By |
|-----------------|-----------------|-------------|
| Grimling Chieftain (south) | Grimlings | Owlbears |
| Sonic Packleader or Ghowlik (east) | Sonic Wolves | Grimlings |
| Skrietat'Cha, Skrietat'Chu, or Owlbear Denmother (north) | Owlbears | Sonic Wolves |

Once the event starts, the first attack wave spawns after a few seconds. Attackers march from the attacking faction's camp toward the defending faction's camp. If the attackers reach their destination, they take control of that location and the defending faction's spawns are replaced.

## The 12-Minute Cycle

After the initial attack, a new attack wave spawns **every 12 minutes**. The attacking faction and target location are chosen **randomly** (50/50 coin flip) between the two factions still present in the zone. The third faction has already been eliminated at this point.

**Pay attention to zone emotes.** They tell you who is attacking and where:

- **Owlbears attacking:** *"Somewhere in the distance, the Owlbear sharpen their claws on boulders..."*
- **Sonic Wolves attacking:** *"Frenzied howling fills the air..."*
- **Grimlings attacking:** *"Hollowshade is abuzz with activity as the Grimlings make preparations..."*

A second emote tells you **where** the defense is being mounted (or if there is no defense):

- *"In the south, the Grimlings nervously scurry about."* -- Grimlings are defending in the south
- *"Owlbear in the north frantically prepare for the assault."* -- Owlbears are defending in the north
- *"The sonic wolves in the east seem disoriented."* -- Sonic wolves are defending in the east

### Undefended Attacks

When a faction holds 2 out of 3 locations, it is **stretched thin**. If the opposing faction attacks, no defenders spawn. You will see an emote like:

> *"Their forces stretched thin due to occupying northern and eastern positions, the Owlbear find themselves unable to muster a defense to stave off the imminent attack."*

An undefended attack **will succeed automatically** unless players kill the attackers before they reach their destination. This is the primary way the zone state changes -- if you are not paying attention, you can lose the position you are farming.

### Attacker and Defender NPCs

Players intervene by killing **attackers** before they reach their target, or by killing **defenders** to help attackers succeed:

| Faction | Attackers | Defenders |
|---------|-----------|-----------|
| Owlbears | a furious owlbear | an owlbear defender |
| Sonic Wolves | a sonic wolf fiend | a sonic defender |
| Grimlings | a grimling invader | a grimling defender |

## Ending the Event

The event can end in two ways:

### 1. Faction Victory (Leader Kill)

If one faction captures **all three locations**, a victory message plays and the faction's **boss leader** spawns:

| Faction Controls All 3 | Leader Spawns |
|------------------------|---------------|
| Owlbears | Skriat'Chakku |
| Sonic Wolves | Gnarlick |
| Grimlings | Prince Garzemort |

Killing the leader **resets the entire zone** back to the default state. The Shar Vahl guards also return to the guard post.

### 2. Natural Reset

If the zone state returns to the default configuration (Owlbears north, Sonic Wolves east, Grimlings south) through normal attack cycles, the event simply stops on its own with no leader spawn.

## Why Farm This Event?

### Rangers -- Archery Item

Rangers want **Owlbears in the east** to farm an archery item. To achieve this, start the event in a way that sends owlbears toward the east, or manipulate the attack cycles to get owlbears to take the eastern position.

### Casters -- Flowing Thought Ranged Item

Casters want **Owlbears at all three locations** to trigger the owlbear victory and spawn **Skriat'Chakku**, who drops a Flowing Thought ranged item.

### Blacksmithing Reagents

Players farm **bears (owlbears) in the south** and **wolves (sonic wolves) in the south** for reagents used in blacksmithing. Getting wolves to the south is harder because it requires manipulating multiple attack cycles from the default state.

## Locking the Zone

If you take the time to get **Sonic Wolves in the south**, you may want to **lock the zone** to prevent other players from ending the event (either intentionally or accidentally) and resetting wolves back to the east.

### How to Lock

1. Get Sonic Wolves into the south through the attack cycle
2. Ensure that a **different faction** (Owlbears or Grimlings) holds the **north, east, or both**
3. Every 12 minutes when an attack wave spawns, **kill the attackers** before they reach their destination

As long as two different factions hold the three locations, no victory condition can trigger. By killing every attack wave, you freeze the zone in place. The wolves stay south, and nobody can end the event without your cooperation.

### What to Watch For

- If you stop paying attention for a cycle, an undefended attack can flip a location
- If one faction ends up controlling all 3 locations, the victory leader spawns. If anyone kills it, the zone resets
- The event also ends naturally if locations return to the default state (Owlbears north, Sonic Wolves east, Grimlings south), so avoid letting the zone drift back to default

### Etiquette

If you see wolves locked in the south, someone is likely actively farming them. Check with them before interfering with the event. Ending the event (by letting a faction take all 3 points and killing the leader) will reset the wolves back to the east and undo potentially significant effort.
